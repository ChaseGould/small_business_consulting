Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project take?

Most pilot projects are delivered in 2 to 6 weeks, depending on scope, data readiness, and the number of systems involved.

How is pricing structured?

We usually work with a fixed-fee pilot, then move to staged implementation or monthly support based on your goals.

Is my data secure?

Security and access control are built in from day one. We use least-privilege access, document data flows, and align with your existing policies.

Do I need new software?

Not always. We prioritize integrating with your current stack first and only recommend new tools when they materially improve reliability or cost.

What tools do you use?

Tool selection depends on your systems. Common categories include CRM integrations, automation platforms, AI APIs, and SMS providers.

How does SMS compliance work?

We design workflows that include consent language, opt-out handling, and message logging aligned with carrier and regulatory requirements.
